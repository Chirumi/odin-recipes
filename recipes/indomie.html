Indomie

Description

This is one of the easiest recipe that anyone could ever make in this world

To be honest, the recipe itself is on the back of the indomie package but I am going to make a unique recipe that enhances the original recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 indomie packet
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil indomie noodles
  2. Add oil
  3. Cook eggs
  4. Add sauce on top of the indomie noodles
  5. Put eggs on top of the noodles and serve hot!
