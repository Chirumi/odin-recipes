Egg Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is originally my mom's recipe that I have decided to modify and somehow make it worse

However, it is excusable since I am not in an asian country at the moment therefore it is difficult to find asian recipes

This recipe is the "broke student" version of my mom's recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Diced chicken
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook Rice
  2. Add oil onto a wok
  3. Cook Egg
  4. Put egg away
  5. Add garlic and cook until golden brown, followed by diced chicken and green onions
  6. Add cooked rice
  7. Add eggs
  8. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce
  9. Add a sprinkle of green onions to finish the dish and serve!
